About Me!

I am Srikanth Dharmavaram (Vaiswanara), a passionate trainer dedicated to empowering individuals through the timeless wisdom of Vedic Astrology and its integration with psychology. My teaching approach combines ancient scriptures like the Bhagavad Gita, Upanishads, and Puranas with practical applications to create a balanced and holistic understanding of life.

My Mission

I believe that Vedic knowledge should be shared freely and without barriers. My mission is to help students:

  • Understand the profound truths of Vedic Astrology and psychology.
  • Strengthen their minds and build resilience, self-restraint, confidence, and contentment.
  • Achieve harmony in modern life by fostering peace and clarity in thought and action.

My Beliefs

I encourage students to focus on the knowledge and insights gained through my courses. There is no need to subscribe, like, or share my videos or page. Instead, I am happiest when students apply what they’ve learned and share their knowledge with others, creating a ripple effect of wisdom.
